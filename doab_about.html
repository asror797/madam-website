About

The story
behind the mic.

Diary of a Breastie was born out of a simple but powerful belief — that every breast cancer survivor's story deserves to be heard, honored, and amplified.

What started as a platform for honest conversation has grown into a full community: a podcast, a newsletter, a resource library, and a wellness coaching practice — all centered on helping survivors and women everywhere heal, thrive, and find joy again.

The mission has always been clear: real stories, real strength. No sugar-coating. No toxic positivity. Just honest, faith-grounded, community-driven support for the journey of survivorship and healing.

Whether you're newly diagnosed, in the thick of treatment, or years into remission — there's a seat at this table for you. You are not alone.
